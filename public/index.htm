Site Analysis

We provide in situ and desktop analysis of site information available to assist in the feasibility and master planning processes. This often includes topographic and climatic analysis as well as regional factors like locally available materials and skills to ensure that all the sustainability criteria are investigated and captured in the holistic planning

Master Planning

We provide a holistic, integrated macro planning service backed by our network of hand-picked network associates, each and every one a specialist in their own field thus ensuring that the most important aspects of the project is maintained.

We have participated in numerous Spatial Development Initiatives to identify and illustrate tourism opportunities in particular regions. We have completed in excess of 30 master plans for regional and national tourism authorities like India, Egypt, UAE and Saudi Arabia and acted as marketing agents for several regional ecotourism drives that involved integrated development methods.

MasterPlanEco – a unique blend of specialist ecoconsultants

Over many years we have developed working relationships with a broad range of specialist associates, who provide value-added services such as impact studies, feasibility assessment, market studies and demand analysis, protected area planning and wildlife management, carbon exchange programmes, building of models and the promotion and procurement of operators and developers for ecotourism destinations.


MasterPlanEco – bringing it all together

Working with these highly experienced and professional associates, Crafford & Crafford Architects has established MasterPlanEco, an international consultancy, to assist eco-tourism and nature based product developers with the master planning of their projects. MasterPlanEco was created to address our growing involvement with International projects that demands fast access to specialist knowledge globally using the successful design integration approach developed by CC Architecture without boundaries.
